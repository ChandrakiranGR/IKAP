# Technique: analogical

## Model
gpt-4o-mini

## User Query
I am a new Northeastern student. How do I enroll in Duo multi-factor authentication?

## Response

Analogy: Enrolling in Duo multi-factor authentication is like adding an extra lock to your front door for added security. It makes sure that only you can access your important information.  
Category: MFA  
Steps:  
1. Go to the Northeastern University Duo enrollment page.  
2. Log in with your Northeastern credentials.  
3. Follow the prompts to set up your Duo account, including choosing your authentication method (like a mobile app or phone call).  
4. Complete the setup by verifying your chosen method.  
If this does not resolve your issue: Contact Northeastern IT Services for further assistance.
