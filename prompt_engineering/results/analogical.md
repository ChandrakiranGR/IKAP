# Technique: analogical

## Model
gpt-4o-mini

## User Query
I am a new Northeastern student. How do I enroll in Duo multi-factor authentication?

## Response

Analogy: Enrolling in Duo multi-factor authentication is like adding a second lock to your door for extra security. It ensures that even if someone has your key, they still need a second way to get in.  
Category: MFA  
Steps:  
1. Go to the Northeastern University Duo enrollment page.  
2. Log in with your Northeastern credentials.  
3. Follow the prompts to set up your device (phone or tablet) for Duo authentication.  
4. Choose your preferred method of authentication (push notification, phone call, or passcode).  
5. Complete the setup by verifying your device.  
If this does not resolve your issue: Contact the IT Service Desk for further assistance.
