# Technique: analogical

## Model
gpt-4o-mini

## User Query
I am a new Northeastern student. How do I enroll in Duo multi-factor authentication?

## Response

Analogy: Enrolling in Duo multi-factor authentication is like adding a second lock to your front door for extra security. It ensures that even if someone has your key (password), they still can't get in without the second lock (authentication method).  
Category: MFA  
Steps:  
1. Go to the Northeastern University Duo enrollment page.  
2. Log in with your Northeastern credentials.  
3. Follow the prompts to set up your Duo account, including selecting your authentication method (like a smartphone app or phone call).  
4. Complete the setup by verifying your chosen method.  
If this does not resolve your issue: Contact the IT Service Desk for further assistance.
