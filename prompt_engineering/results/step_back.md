## User Query
I am a new Northeastern student. How do I enroll in Duo multi-factor authentication?

## Response

High-level approach: Enrolling in Duo multi-factor authentication involves setting up your account and linking your devices for secure access.

Steps:

1. Visit the official enrollment page for Duo multi-factor authentication provided by Northeastern.

2. Follow the prompts to log in with your Northeastern credentials.

3. Choose the type of device you want to use for authentication (e.g., smartphone, tablet, or landline).

4. Follow the instructions to download the Duo Mobile app if you're using a smartphone or tablet, or set up your landline for calls or texts.

5. Complete the setup by verifying your device through the app or the method you selected.
