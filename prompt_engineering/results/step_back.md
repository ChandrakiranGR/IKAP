## User Query
I am a new Northeastern student. How do I enroll in Duo multi-factor authentication?

## Response

High-level approach: Enrolling in Duo multi-factor authentication involves setting up your account and registering your device for added security.

Steps:

1. Visit the official enrollment page for Duo multi-factor authentication provided by Northeastern.
2. Log in using your Northeastern credentials.
3. Follow the prompts to select your authentication method (e.g., mobile app, phone call, or SMS).
4. If using the mobile app, download the Duo Mobile app on your smartphone and scan the QR code provided during the setup.
5. Complete the registration process by verifying your device through the method you selected.
