# Technique: v1_system_prompt

## Model
gpt-4o-mini

## User Query
I am a new Northeastern student. How do I enroll in Duo multi-factor authentication?

## Response

Category: MFA  
Likely cause: You need to enroll in Duo MFA to secure your account, which is a common requirement for accessing university resources.  
Clarifying question(s): Are you trying to enroll using a specific device or operating system?  
Steps:  
1. Visit the Duo enrollment page provided by Northeastern (check your university's IT services website for the link).  
2. Follow the prompts to set up your preferred authentication method (like a mobile app, phone call, or text message).  
3. Complete the enrollment by verifying your chosen method.  
Preventive tip: Ensure your mobile device is connected to the internet during the enrollment process.  
If it still fails: Contact Northeastern IT Services for assistance and provide details about your device type, operating system, and any error messages you encounter.
